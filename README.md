# Hospital Performance Dashboard

An interactive and data-driven dashboard built to analyze hospital performance, patient demographics, treatment costs, and revenue generation. This project helps healthcare administrators track key performance indicators (KPIs) and optimize hospital operations.

## 🔑 Key Metrics (KPIs)
The dashboard tracks the following major clinical and financial metrics:
* **Total Patients:** 3,612 (Total admissions analyzed)
* **Total Treatment Cost:** ₹45.4 Cr (Overall cost incurred for treatments)
* **Total Revenue:** ₹37.7 Cr (Total revenue generated by the hospital)

---

## 📈 Key Insights & Visualizations Included

### 1. Financial Analysis
* **Sum of Treatment Cost vs. Department:** A bar chart showcasing the distribution of expenses across major departments like Cardiology, General Medicine, Neurology, and Oncology.
* **Revenue Tracking:** High-level visualization of the hospital's financial health.

### 2. Operational & Patient Trends
* **Count of Recovery Status vs. Admission Year:** Tracks patient recovery trends across multiple years (2022, 2023, 2024) to monitor healthcare quality over time.
* **Demographic Analysis:** Insights into patient distribution based on Age, Gender, and specific Diseases.
* **Department-wise Performance:** Filterable views to analyze metrics for individual hospital sectors.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
* **Data Source:** Cleaned Hospital Dataset (`Patient_Master`, `Mastersheet`, `Treatment_Cost`, etc.)
* **Platform:** Google Sheets / Microsoft Excel (Formula-driven KPI cards and custom Indian Currency Formatting `₹ Cr`)
* **Formatting:** Custom Number Formatting for high-value financial data.

## 💡 How to Use the Dashboard
1. Open the spreadsheet file or view the attached PDF.
2. Use the **Department**, **Gender**, and **Disease** dropdown filters at the top to slice and dice the data.
3. Observe how the KPIs and charts dynamic shift based on your selection.
